**Aṣẹ** refers to the vital power to make things happen—authority, command, and the force that enables words, rituals, and intention to take effect.

In Yorùbá thought, **Aṣẹ** is not simply “power” as domination, but **power as alignment**: speech, character, and action working with destiny and the sacred order.
