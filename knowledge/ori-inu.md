NOTES

Orí Inú refers to the inner consciousness within a person in Yorùbá thought.

It represents the deeper awareness and moral intuition that guides a person toward
living in alignment with their chosen destiny.

While Orí represents the destiny chosen before birth, Orí Inú is the inner voice
that helps a person maintain balance, wisdom, and good character.
